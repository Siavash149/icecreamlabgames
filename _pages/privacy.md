This Privacy Policy describes how IcecreamLab Games ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile games and applications (collectively, the "Games"). By using our Games, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide

When you create an account or play our Games, you may provide a username or display name, optional profile information, and in-app chat messages in games with chat features.

### Information collected automatically

When you use our Games, we automatically collect device information (device type, OS version, unique device identifiers), gameplay data (sessions, scores, match history), network information (IP address, connection type), and crash and performance data to help us fix bugs.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve our Games, enable online multiplayer matchmaking, display leaderboards and player statistics, detect and prevent cheating or abuse, respond to support requests, show relevant in-game advertising via Google AdMob, and analyze usage patterns to improve game design.

## 3. Third-Party Services

Our Games use the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

- **Google Play Services** — for app distribution, authentication, and leaderboards. [Google Privacy Policy ↗](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google AdMob** — to display advertisements within our free Games. [AdMob Privacy ↗](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)
- **Unity Analytics** — for gameplay analytics and crash reporting. [Unity Privacy Policy ↗](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- **Firebase** — for backend services including real-time database and authentication. [Firebase Privacy ↗](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## 4. Advertising

Some of our Games display ads powered by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests using your advertising ID (GAID). You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device's settings under Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

## 5. In-App Purchases

Some Games offer optional in-app purchases processed through Google Play. We do not store your payment information — all transactions are handled by Google Play's secure payment system.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your account and gameplay data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our Games are rated for users aged 3 and above on Google Play. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## 8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
